The Kampala Hotel

The hotel was founded by Kisekka Yusuf in 2004. Our hotel is known to have the most hospitable and financially affordable services.

Vision

We aim at providing quality services to the locals and foreigners that reach out to us. These quality services include transportation from the airport if you are a traveller, feeding, spacious rooms, gym, Spa and an overflow swimming pool.

Hotel Branches

We have branches in four main cities around the country which are Kampala, Mbarara, Arua and Mbale. Kampala branch is located at Lumumba avenue plot 5. Mbarara branch is located along Mbarara-Bushenyi highway opposite Stanbic bank Mbarara branch. Arua branch is located 6 miles along Arua-Koboko road. Mbale branch is located along Mbale-Tororo road opposite YY bus park.

Spa

Our spa offers facials, waxing and massages and is always open from 8am to 5pm on weekdays and from 10am to 8pm on weekends.

The gym

We have a well stocked gym and trained gym assistants that are available in the morning from 7am to 10am and evening from 4pm to 9pm. We also provide membership cards to the loyal gym customers that are provided at a discounted price.

Swimming pool

Kampala hotel provides kids pools and half olympic sized pools at all our branches. All our pools have certified on-duty life guards to ensure customer safety.

Some of our kitchen stuff

All our kitchen stuff have experience and skills acquired from the best institutions in the country.
